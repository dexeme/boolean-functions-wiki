\(x^{2^s+1} + u^{2^k-1}x^{2^{ik}+2^{mk+s}}\)
\(n = pk,\) | \(\gcd(k, 3) = \gcd(s, 3k) = 1,\) | \(p \in \{3, 4\},\) | \(i = sk \pmod p,\) | \(m = p - i,\) | \(n \geq 12,\) | \(u\) primitive in \(\mathbb{F}_{2^n}^*\)

C3

\(sx^{q+1} + x^{2^i+1} + x^{q(2^i+1)} + cx^{2^iq+1} + c^qx^{2^i+q}\)
\(q = 2^m,\) | \(n = 2m,\) | \(\gcd(i, m) = 1,\) | \(c \in \mathbb{F}_{2^n},\) | \(s \in \mathbb{F}_{2^n} \setminus \mathbb{F}_q,\) | \(X^{2^i+1} + cX^{2^i} + c^qX + 1\) has no solution \(x\) s.t. \(x^{q+1} = 1\)

C7-C9

\(ux^{2^s+1} + u^{2^k}x^{2^{-k}+2^{k+s}} + vx^{2^{-k}+1} + wu^{2^k+1}x^{2^s+2^{k+s}}\)
\(n = 3k,\) | \(\gcd(k, 3) = \gcd(s, 3k) = 1,\) | \(v, w \in \mathbb{F}_{2^k},\) | \(vw \neq 1,\) | \(3|(k+s),\) | \(u\) primitive in \(\mathbb{F}_{2^n}^*\)

C10

\((x+x^{2^m})^{2^k+1} + u'(ux+u^{2^m}x^{2^m})^{(2^k+1)2^i} + u(x+x^{2^m})(ux+u^{2^m}x^{2^m})\)
\(n=2m,\) | \(m \geq 2\) even, | \(\gcd(k,m)=1\) and \(i \geq 2\) even, | \(u\) primitive in \(\mathbb{F}_{2^n}^*,\) | \(u' \in \mathbb{F}_{2^m}\) not a cube

\(n=km,\) | \(m > 1,\) | \(\gcd(n,i)=1,\) | \(L(x) = \sum_{j=0}^{k-1} a_j x^{2^{jm}}\) satisfies conditions in Theorem 6.3 of [7]

C12

\(n=2m,\) | \(q=2^m,\) | \(\gcd(m,i)=1,\) | \(t(x)=u^qx+x^qu,\) | \(X^{2^i+1}+aX+b\) has no solution over \(\mathbb{F}_{2^m}\)
